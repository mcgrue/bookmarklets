Pucatrade sending address aggregator

This bookmarklet will collect all of the addresses you need to send cards to as listed on your Active Trades page.

  1. The bookmarklet - copy this link into your bookmarks.
  2. Login to pucatrade and click on https://pucatrade.com/trades/active
  3. Click on the bookmark while on that page. The list of addresses you need to mail to has now been put into a text field for you to copy around.

    (Refreshing the page will return it to its normal state.)

  4. Share and enjoy!
